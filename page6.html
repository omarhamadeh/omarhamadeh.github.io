"Being immersed in the world of visual arts, performance and psychology at such a young age, I completed 3 degrees of such, at the Lebanese American University in 2019.  My most significant work explores psycho-political trauma within gender, identity and communication. Recently, I had spent 2 years expanding my career in photography, working in Copenhagen’s Fashion photography industry with international stylists and visual artists. I have also directed and edited music video films for Universal Music Group signed artist Mougleta, and London & LA-based queer artists such as KHANSA, and TYRO.  I have also been hired as a freelance Producer & Creative Director for many projects in Beirut by local agencies like Black Howl and Spotless Mind Productions. After three years of preparation, my debut music & visual collection, “CATHARSIS” will premiere in a couple of months–– a visual EP that consists of three main tracks that are accompanied by a film. Inspired by the works of Björk, BANKS, SOPHIE, David Bowie and Marina Abramovic, my performance persona, formerly known as "VENUS", makes music a psychotropic space journey into an electronic-rock and trip-pop planet of immense atmospheric pressure. I can't wait for you to enjoy the ride."

Omar Hamadeh
