"Mother"

mother,

i serenade you, open-chested

wounds unhealed 

cells unfed

to ask you:

will you look after me?

when the world collapses

underneath my bed?

will you serenade me?

beneath the unfathomable

complexity of my flesh?

i purge,

to the self-handicapped woman

whose once golden shoe-straps distorted rural heteropatriarchal heads.

i purge,

to the valiant female who only fears her own reflection in the mirror, completely unclothed, vulnerably wretched.

are you listening?

have you numbed your eyes deaf to filter misogynist male attempts?

haven't you taught me to persevere within a circle of draught, water my roots with my own skin-shed?

do not collapse, mother

age is a temple waiting to mourn your death

do not collapse, mother

you are safe here

underneath this bed.

"Friends"

twisted ankles

concealed under-eyes

my eyelashes shed

crystallized oceans

they don't listen

they don't understand

they play pretend

for humor or command

drowning in glitter

falling in sight

the wild ones stay

the weak ones fight

deluded presumptions

manifested defense

equivocal egos

undisclosed offense

they bled my hair

they drank my bath

those tears of blood

are lifeline paths

find me in

a garden of flowers

listening to birds

howling for hours

charging my heart

to self-empower

washing my skin

with earth's shower

Blindfold

I speak into ears, deaf.⁣

I operatically howl at cells, unfed.⁣

My lava has become a drought.⁣

My tectonic plates have shed.⁣

⁣

I envy your disguise of ignorance.⁣

It's the best theatre I have ever seen.⁣

You claimed to look right through me,⁣

But clearer was your pride, than your sight will ever be.⁣

⁣

They say good things take time.⁣

But great things, that, was never a scene.

I had torn off my blindfold,⁣

Right before you faked to love me.⁣

⁣

This is not my revenge.⁣

For vengeance, I will never seek.⁣

You were always forgiven, my love.⁣

Because Love, is what I will always bleed.

"See"

failure to see

the dotted patterns of loss

when you only transcribe

over fearful gender expressions

mediated from a communal agreement

this shouldn't be your performance

this isn't your stage

this is the universe's,

glaring at us gazing into its stellar vastness

stargazing isn't really a sport,

it is a moment, of humility.

but i believe that's of great sacrifice for you,

since the only gaze you see is a reflection on lead glass.

"Emancipate Me"

They told me running through the wild, and devoting a whole identity to wanderlust will emancipate my most repressed personas. They told me that walking on brick barefoot with peeled scabs and bloody sweat will bring me an eternity of wealth. Where is your stealth now, sir? What was so profoundly important for you to outweigh my stamina over a perfectly rational self-concept? Is it fascinating for you, sir? To watch me tip-toe into the woods now in fear of the pandemonium of my high heels to resonate with your most delicate post-traumatic wavelengths? Did you purposely kill all those chirping birds to pursue a life of your own where Balenciaga is an ideal to seek of a façade for this falsely inherited bourgeoisie lifestyle? Your glass windows will soon collapse, sir, if you guard them for too long, perhaps you will also collapse soon, too —under my deathwish.

"Relapse"

And I guess I've known it all along: I'll never be able to purge you out of my system. Molten rock and lava exude from my eyelids, as I watch you unhindered, unbothered, unrequited. I told myself that I have heard my pain heal. I told myself that devoting an entire lifestyle for a cruel youth running through the wild, was not productive. Tonight I have relapsed. Into a cycle of contemplation again. Little did I ever contemplate quietly, without the tumult sound of your conclusive voice informing me that we were "unfit". Little did I ever doubt the words that uttered through your perfectly penciled lips across my bleeding eardrums. Doubt is not what I seek. What I seek is: a clarity. I'm afraid. My actions have mirrored the ones I sought from you. I fall into arms unarmed, unknown, unharmed. Now, I fear my own inflicted harm towards softness. I fear of the unconsciously adapted defense mechanisms that my body resorted to in our instances of traumatic dispute. I envy your bliss for ignorance. Mine has yet to come —my curiosity is handling the wheel right now. It's too early for me to sacrifice what I value the most in me. I'm too young. And you are not worth it. You never were. But —I still relapse. And I still L—

"Empathy"

shifting focal points

conduct a tragedy

when greeted in category

shapeshift rapidly

let the counter-arguments sing

above their melodies

follow their path

find your destiny

don't be yourself

unlearn yourself

fight and flight simultaneously

refract specificity

be an evolving unknown

until you feel empathy.
