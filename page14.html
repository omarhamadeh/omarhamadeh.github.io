"Pretension Detention"
You play along your scripted lines pretty well. I understand your pursuit of a standardized self-ideal of perfection, alright. I do understand the adversity within a social labyrinth of communication that entitles you towards a constant display of invulnerability. Not only does it entitle you, but also condescend you. Ridicule you. Shame you, for any display of your weaknesses. I understand the past of your evidently ostentatious behavior, and why you might think it's 'sexy'. But I do think it's cowardly. Your desire for status is cowardly. Your desire for superiority is cowardly. Your negation towards the emotionally sensible is cowardly. Your perception of cowardice is cowardly.
Let's play. Let's fool around. Let's enjoy our presence, regardless of each other's dangerous standardized ideals. Let's laugh, cry and sing, and experience mindless behavior. Just for a moment. I won't hold it against you or my judgement of your entity -- I promise.
